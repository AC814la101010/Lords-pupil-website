Everyone is tempted no matter their title, status, gender, etc. Being tempted doesn't mean that your faith is weak, its the ability of either falling into it or asking God to get you through it.

Temptation?

Temptation is the feeling of being pulled into to disobeying God. It's the part in a flim where you have the devil on one shoulder and an angel on the other. The devil tries to feed your mind with fleshly desires that are not of God.
